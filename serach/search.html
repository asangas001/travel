Relaxing Riverside Stroll and Culinary Exploration in Location 1

9am - 1pm

34 F'

Arrival in Sigiriya

  • Morning: Arrive at Sigiriya via either Colombo or Polonnaruwa.
  • Afternoon: Check into your accommodation.
  • Evening: Relax and enjoy a leisurely evening exploring the local surroundings. Try some traditional Sri Lankan cuisine at a nearby restaurant.

Sigiriya Rock Fortress

9am - 1pm

34 F'

Sigiriya Rock Fortress

  • Morning: Visit the iconic Sigiriya Rock Fortress, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Start early to avoid the heat and crowds.
  • Afternoon: After descending from the fortress, visit the Sigiriya Museum to delve deeper into the history and significance of this ancient site.
  • Evening: Enjoy a sunset safari at Minneriya National Park, renowned for its elephant gatherings, just a short drive from Sigiriya.

Cultural Exploration

9am - 1pm

34 F'

  • Morning: Explore the ancient city of Polonnaruwa, another UNESCO World Heritage Site, known for its well-preserved ruins of ancient Sri Lankan civilization.
  • Afternoon: Visit the Dambulla Cave Temple, a complex of cave temples adorned with stunning Buddhist murals and statues.
  • Evening: Return to Sigiriya and spend your evening at leisure, perhaps indulging in a traditional Ayurvedic massage to relax after a day of exploration.
